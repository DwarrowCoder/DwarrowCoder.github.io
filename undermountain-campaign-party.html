Kyras

A half-elf druid from the Dalelands, Kyras is very much a man of two worlds. Pulled between civilization and the wilds, between human and elf, between growth and destruction. After guiding a group of clerics to the city of Waterdeep, he has joined a newly formed party in order to try something new.
