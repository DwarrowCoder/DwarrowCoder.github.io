Orcs

There are several varieties of orc found in the world of Toril.

Gray Orcs

Forgotten Realms Wiki

Use the standard orc for most features unless otherwise indicated.
Ability Score Modifiers: +2 Strength, –2 Intelligence, +2 Wisdom, –2 Charisma.
Base Speed: Gray orcs have a base speed of 40 feet. Weapon Familiarity: Orcs are always proficient with greataxes and long bows, and treat any weapon with the word “orc” in its name as a martial weapon. Replaces the normal orc Weapon Familiarity.
Scent: The gray orc has the standard scent ability.
Regions: The Hordelands, Thesk

Mountain Orcs

Forgotten Realms Wiki

Use the standard orc for most features unless otherwise indicated.
Weapon Familiarity: Orcs are always proficient with greataxes and javelins, and treat any weapon with the word “orc” in its name as a martial weapon. Replaces the normal orc Weapon Familiarity.
Regions: Chessenta, The Moonsea, The North, Thesk, Underdark (Northdark), Vaasa.
Random Height: 5'8" + 2d10" (male), 5'0" + 2d10" (female)
Random Weight: 150 lb. + (2d6 × [height roll]) lb. (male), 115 lb. + (2d6 × [height roll]) lb. (female)

Orog (Deep Orc)

Forgotten Realms Wiki

Use the standard orc for most features unless otherwise indicated.
Ability Score Modifiers: +6 Strength, –2 Dexterity, -2 Wisdom, +2 Charisma.
Superior Darkvision: Orogs have superior darkvision, allowing them to see perfectly in the dark up to 120 feet. Replaces Darkvision.
Weapon Familiarity: Orcs are always proficient with greataxes and throwing axes, and treat any weapon with the word “orc” in its name as a martial weapon. Replaces the normal orc Weapon Familiarity.
Light Blindness: As deep underground dwellers naturally, orogs suffer from light blindness. Abrupt exposure to any bright light blinds orogs for 1 round. On subsequent rounds, they are dazzled as long as they remain in the affected area. Replaces Light Sensitivity.
Natural Armor: Orogs have +2 natural armor.
Resistances: Orogs have cold resistance 5 and fire resistance 5.
Regions: The Moonsea, The North, Underdark (Northdark).
Random Height: 5'10" + 2d10" (male), 5'2" + 2d10" (female)
Random Weight: 160 lb. + (2d6 × [height roll]) lb. (male), 125 lb. + (2d6 × [height roll]) lb. (female)

Tanarukks

Forgotten Realms Wiki

Use the standard orc for most features unless otherwise indicated.
Ability Score Modifiers: +4 Strength, +2 Dexterity, -2 Wisdom, -4 Charisma.
Type: Tanarukks are outsiders with the native and orc subtype.
Base Speed: Tanarukks have a base speed of 20 feet, but their speed is never modified by armor or encumbrance.
Resistances: Tanarukks have fire resistance 10.
Natural Armor: Tanarukks have +4 natural armor.
Spell Resistance: Tanarukks possess spell resistance (SR) equal to 14 plus their total number of class levels.
Control Flame (Sp): Tanarukks can cause a nonmagical fire within 10 feet of them to diminish to the level of coals or flare to the brightness of daylight and double the normal radius of illumination. This ability does not change the heat output or fuel consumption of the fuel source, lasts 5 minutes, and may be done once per day. They use this ability as a 5th-level sorcerer.
Natural Weapons (Ex): A tanarukk has a bite attack that deals 1d6 points of damage.
Remove Weapon Familiarity and Light Sensitivity.
Regions: The North, Underdark (Northdark).
Random Height: 5'10" + 2d10" (male), 5'2" + 2d10" (female)
Random Weight: 160 lb. + (2d6 × [height roll]) lb. (male), 125 lb. + (2d6 × [height roll]) lb. (female)
