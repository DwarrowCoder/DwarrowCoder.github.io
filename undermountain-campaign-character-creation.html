Step 1: Determine Attribute Scores

You can choose between two different methods to generate your attribute scores, the Standard Array or Random Generation.

Standard Array: Assign an 8, 10, 12, 13, 14, and a 15 to your character's attributes.

Random Generation: Roll four six-sided dice, remove the lowest dice, and record the total of the other three dice. Repeat this five times and then assign the results to your character's attributes. You can reroll your attributes if the average of your attribute modifiers is less than +1.

Step 8: Select Background Traits

Choose two traits, plus one if you select a drawback, from the following categories: Basic - Combat, Basic - Faith, Basic - Magic, Basic - Social, Race, Regional, and Religion. Traits should be tied to your backstory in some way and are subject to DM approval. All traits should be from different categories.
